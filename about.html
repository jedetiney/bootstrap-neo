About AeroIntel

At AeroIntel, we are the bridge between the realms of technology and intelligence, combining a passion for satellite imagery with a commitment to serving the ever-evolving needs of our clients. Our journey is shaped by a dynamic fusion of expertise, vision, and innovation.

Meet Our Visionary Leadership

Headed by the visionary Jay Patel, a luminary with over 8 years of unrivaled experience in the realm of cybersecurity, AeroIntel sets its compass on excellence and precision. Jay's profound understanding of the intricate world of data security has been pivotal in our approach to safeguarding the information we handle and what we extract from it.

Our Diverse Team of Experts

Our strength lies in our team—a closely-knit group of experienced professionals who bring a rich tapestry of skills and backgrounds to the table. With members hailing from diverse fields such as journalism, law, and computer science, AeroIntel thrives on a blend of knowledge and expertise. This collective intelligence fuels our commitment to providing actionable insights and open-source intelligence from satellite imagery.

Our Mission

At AeroIntel, our mission is simple yet profound: to offer our clients the power of knowledge derived from the vantage point of the skies. We aim to enable businesses, governments, and individuals to make informed decisions by harnessing the vast potential of satellite imagery. Our dedication to quality, accuracy, and innovation sets us apart.

Why Choose AeroIntel

Choosing AeroIntel means choosing a partner committed to excellence. Our team's diverse backgrounds and skills ensure a well-rounded approach to problem-solving, enabling us to deliver comprehensive solutions. Whether you're a government agency seeking security insights, a business striving for a competitive edge, or an individual curious to explore the world from a new perspective, we have the expertise and the passion to empower your journey.
